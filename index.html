Tayab: The Eternal Protector of Mars

President of Mars

For over a thousand years, President Tayab has ruled Mars, not just as a leader but as its eternal protector. Blessed with incredible magical powers and the wisdom of millennia, Tayab has been locked in a century-long war against ancient demons seeking to plunge Mars into darkness. Though these demons are relentless, Tayab's resolve is unbreakable. Wielding magic that can bend reality, he fights every day, vanquishing his foes and keeping Mars safe. Despite the endless battles, his vow remains the same: "I will fight until the last demon falls & I love Cigeretes." His people know—Mars will never fall as long as Tayab stands.

Alliances with the Celestial Titans Across Galaxies

      Xyron, Titan of Light

    • In the early days, Tayab sought Xyron, Titan of Light, for the power to fight darkness. Impressed by Tayab’s courage, Xyron granted him control over light, enabling him to burn away the demonic forces with beams of radiant energy.

      Mortha, Titan of Shadows

    • To understand his enemies better, Tayab met Mortha, Titan of Shadows. Mortha revealed the demons' weaknesses, giving Tayab insight into their strategies and enabling him to strike at their core.

      The Pact with Lucifer

    • In a desperate move, Muhammad Tayab ventured into Hell to forge a pact with Lucifer. Impressed by Tayab's boldness, Lucifer granted him forbidden knowledge of the demons’ true nature. In return, Tayab agreed not to interfere with Hell’s realm.
